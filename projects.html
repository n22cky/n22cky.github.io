i am not a robot

I am not a robot, March 2025, etching on mirrors

created for an independent study during a fourth-year drawing course at the MIWSFPA, this project aims to explore the intersecting relationship between cybersecurity and identity. as humans are naturally drawn to their reflection, the viewer is compelled to see themselves through phrases evocative of the human experience which have been distorted to resemble various CAPTCHA techniques.
